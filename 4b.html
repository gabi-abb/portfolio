Pulse of The Machine

Our team was challenged to produce and showcase a fully video mapped 1:1 architectural model, which explored the prefix TRANS. We researched and explored the term Transhumanism in the context of human centric technologies since the emergence of human life. spanning technologies such as the invention of glasses, to the embedded body modifications and implanted microchips.
